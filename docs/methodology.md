# Methodology

## Project Overview

This project is inspired by preventive quality planning and supply chain decision-making processes used in industrial environments.

The objective is to determine whether a Standard PMQ Process or a Modified PMQ Process should be used based on business and operational criteria.

---

## Decision Criteria

The decision is based on four key factors:

1. Part novelty
2. Risk level
3. Supplier availability
4. Expected efficiency gains

---

## Modified PMQ Process

The Modified PMQ Process can be applied when:

- The part is not completely new
- Risk level is B or C
- A qualified supplier already exists
- Time and cost advantages are expected

---

## Standard PMQ Process

The Standard PMQ Process should be used when:

- The part is new
- Risk level is A
- No qualified supplier exists
- No measurable efficiency gains are expected
